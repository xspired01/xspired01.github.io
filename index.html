NFL Team Win Projector

Want to Predict the Future?

Want to know how your team will do next season? Should you buy tickets? This handy web application will show you how well your team will do next year and whether you should buy tickets? Enter team name or location and hit enter.

The predictions are based on Bill James' creation of the Pythagorean Theorem of Baseball. Instead of using distance, this theorem points scored and points allowed are used to calculate a team's baseline winning percentage and multiplying by the number of games.
